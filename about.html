Our Story

We're an artisanal, from-scratch doughnut shop and board game taproom on the north side of Indianapolis that believes in the importance of time well-spent. Since our doors opened in 2019, we've been focused on bringing people together - whether it's sharing a box of our bakery's newest creations or tossing back a pint while crushing your frustrated friends in Risk, we strive to create experiences that matter, in an environment embracing of all. Come check us out, and see what all of the hype is about!
