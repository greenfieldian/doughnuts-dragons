Our Complexity Scale

We have a lot of games to choose from. To make it easier to dive into games you aren’t familiar with, we’ve developed a complexity scale, so you know what to expect when you open the box. Each starburst represents an additional level of complexity, from a simple, easyto-learn Level 1 game all the way to a “put your damn phone down, there are still 10 pages left” Level 6 game. More complex games aren’t necessarily longer, just more involved. If you’re on the fence, our servers are always happy to help you decide!

Game Mechanics Badges

Area Control

Area Control games are games in which players compete for specific areas of the board to win, often by placing units or resources. Control of an area typically grants points or some kind of award or benefit

Dice Rolling

Any Game using or relying upon dice to facilitate gameplay qualifies as a dice rolling game

Hand Management

A game utilizing the hand management mechanic is one in which players gain advantages based on the order in which they play out or collect the cards, tiles, etc. in their hands.

Player Elimination

Watch out! In a player elimination game, players can be permanently eliminated prior to the conclusion of the game.

Drafting

In a drafting game, players take turns making selections out of a larger shared pool of choices.

Set Collection

As the name suggests, if players are encouraged to collect items to form a set, you’re playing a set collection game!

Tile Placement

A tile placement game is any game in which players place tiles or pieces as the primary method of developing the game.

Pickup and Deliver

Special delivery! A game in which players are encouraged to pick up an item or items and bring them to other locations is a pickup and deliver game.

Bidding/Wagering

Bidding/wagering games are games in which players compete through bids or bets, leveraging risk to gain an advantage.

Deduction

Games in which the objective requires players to speculate with limited information and reach an overarching conclusion.

Deckbuilding

Deckbuilding In deckbuilding games, players construct a unique deck from the game’s components and face off with their creations.
